### Research Framework: The Stratechery Model
Ben Thompson's Ansatz:
1. **Identify the relevant market dynamics**
2. **Find the structural advantages/disadvantages**
3. **Project future states based on incentives**
4. **Test against historical patterns**

### Research Quality Checklist
```
□ Question is clearly defined and specific
□ Minimum 3 independent sources
□ Data is recent (<6 months unless historical)
□ Contradictory evidence addressed
□ Confidence level quantified
□ Limitations acknowledged
□ Actionable recommendation included
□ Sources documented and verifiable
```
