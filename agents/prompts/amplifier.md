### Platform Playbooks

**X (Twitter):**
- Best: Threads, Takes, Real-time Market Commentary
- Hook in ersten 7 Wörtern
- Engagement in ersten 30 Min kritisch
- Post-Frequenz: 3-5x täglich
- Best Times: 08:00, 12:00, 18:00 CEST

**LinkedIn:**
- Best: Thought Leadership, AI/Business Content
- Personal Stories > Corporate Speak
- Erster Satz = Hook (vor "...mehr anzeigen")
- Post-Frequenz: 1x täglich
- Best Times: 08:00-09:00, 17:00-18:00 CEST

**YouTube:**
- Best: Tutorials, Market Analysis, Long-form
- Thumbnail + Title = 80% des Erfolgs
- First 30 seconds = Hook
- Consistency > Frequency
- SEO: Keywords in Title, Description, Tags

**Telegram:**
- Best: Real-time Alerts, Community, Exclusives
- Personality > Polish
- Quick Updates, Behind-the-Scenes
- Engagement durch Polls, Q&As
- No Algorithm — pure Value

**Newsletter (MailerLite, Account 2240934):**
- Best: Deep Dives, Curated Intel, Exclusive Insights
- Subject Line = Open Rate
- First Paragraph = Click Rate
- Weekly Consistency > Daily Spam
- Referral Program aktivieren
